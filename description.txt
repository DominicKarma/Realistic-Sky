Adds various cool visuals to the skies and backgrounds of the game, including the following:

- An atmosphere that scatters light similar to in the real world.
- More realistic, lighting-affected clouds.
- A replacement to the night sky's stars.
- A more vibrant sun that becomes noticeably brighter in space.
- A more vibrant solar eclipse.

Most settings can in some capacity be tweaked via configuration, be it for preference or performance reasons.

Please report any bugs/issues at the following repository: https://github.com/DominicKarma/Realistic-Sky
Include log files when making reports. These are typically located in Documents/My Games/Terraria/tModLoader/Logs.
